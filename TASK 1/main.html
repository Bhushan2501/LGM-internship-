Variants

Mustang Fastback

Ford Mustang GT Fastback 5.0L v8 is the top model in the Mustang lineup. It returns a certified mileage of 7.9 kmpl. This GT Fastback 5.0L v8 variant comes with an engine putting out 396 bhp @ 6500 rpm and 515 Nm @ 4250 rpm of max power and max torque respectively. Ford Mustang GT Fastback 5.0L v8 is available in Automatic transmission.

Mustang Mach-1

The new Mach 1 is powered by a 480-hp 5.0-liter V-8 with a standard six-speed manual transmission from the Shelby GT350, although a 10-speed automatic with paddle shifters is available.

Mustang GT500

Mustang GT500 boasts 760 horses of supercharged-V-8 mayhem, but it also possesses the athleticism of smaller and lighter sports cars. At the center of the excitement is the Shelby's supercharged 5.2-liter V-8, which plays a thrilling soundtrack through its bazooka-like exhaust pipes. A manual transmission isn't available, but the seven-speed dual-clutch automatic operates damn near telepathically.
